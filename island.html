Island of Halki

Halki is a Greek island and municipality in the Dodecanese archipelago in the Aegean Sea, 4 mi west of Rhodes and with an area of 11sq mi. Characterized by UNESCO as the Island of Peace and Friendship, Halki is an artistc island with neoclassical mansions spread around the harbour. Niborio is the picturesque capital and harbour of the island with an authentic identity and atmosphere. Halki is an island with great tradition, remarkable historical monuments and unspoiled beaches ready to be discovered.
