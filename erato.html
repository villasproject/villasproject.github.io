Villetta Erato

Erato was built in 1870 as a typical fisherman house using arch elements. Fully renovated in 2019, the property features sea, city and mountain views. Villetta Erato provides accomodation in Halki with free WiFi and sea views, providing a patio and garden.This holiday home includes 1 bedroom, a living room, satelite flat-screen TV, an equipped kitchenette, bathroom and shower.
