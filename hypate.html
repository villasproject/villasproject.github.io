Villa Hypate

Hypate is located in Halki town, just 25 metres from the port. The house was built in 1890 and renovated under South Aegean architectural guidelines, reflecting the island traddition. It opens to a patio with scenic views of the surrounding area. it is decorated with traditional, yet stylish way. Hypate consists of a spacious living room, fully equipped kitchen and flat-screen TV with satelite channels. The bathroom, located right outside the villa, features a shower and free toiletries.
